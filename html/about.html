Proud locally-owned business since 1891

Ever since our founder Casey David created byteCafe in 1891, our mission has been to provide high quality organic coffee. Sourced straight from locally-owned sustainable farms across South America, our famous coffees provide a taste unmatched across the Northeast. We continuously seek to improve our selection of foods and drinks while keeping true to our quality standards.

Our leadership

Today's byteCafe is in the capable hands of Taylor Evans. Evans is a true coffee aficionado, having graduated with a Master's degree in Coffee Science from the University of California, Davis. Evan's commitment to making the greatest coffee has led him to travel the seven continents in search for the hidden coffee-making secrets of the world. It's on the backs of all the great coffee brewers of history, that byteCafe is able to present its selections of coffees, drinks, and delicious foods.

At the heart of byteCafe is our community - the coffee lovers that made our business possible. We seek to contribute a peaceful relaxing atmosphere where people can come in to hang out, study, and enjoy a nice sip of coffee. Feel free to visit at any time.
